# Privacy Policy — Sudoku

_Last updated: 14 June 2026_

Sudoku ("the app"), published by Tertiary Infotech, is designed to be completely
private.

## Data we collect

**None.** The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. It requests
no Android permissions and has no internet access.

## Data stored on your device

Your game progress, scores, statistics, and settings are stored only on your device
(in the app's private storage). This information never leaves your phone and is not
accessible to us or any third party. You can erase it at any time from
**Settings → Statistics → clear history**, or by uninstalling the app.

## Children's privacy

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

## Third parties

The app contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs.
